Additionally, Squid Game (오징어 게임) is in the input list and 'Squid Game: The Challenge' is a direct spin-off, which also warrants a penalty.

Overall Critique

This recommendation set has a solid conceptual foundation — Silo, Tulsa King, The Recruit and Yellowjackets are genuinely appropriate picks for this taste profile — but it is severely undermined by three structural failures: a duplicate Yellowjackets entry with a phantom placeholder show preceding it, a Fauda entry that names an input-show in its title in direct violation of the no-overlap rule, and a mid-reason pivot that abandons one show to recommend another. Metadata accuracy suffers from the copy-paste errors, and the overall diversity is weak given the heavy 2021–2023 US clustering. With cleanup and replacement of the broken entries, the underlying recommendations would score considerably higher.
